Theorem 3.3.2.

  1. If \(r\) and \(s\) are any numbers and \(f\) and \(g\) are differentiable at \(x\text{,}\) then \((rf+sg)'(x)=rf'(x)+sg'(x)\text{.}\)
  2. If \(f\) and \(g\) are differentiable at \(x\text{,}\) then \((fg)'(x)=f'(x)g(x)+f(x)g'(x)\text{.}\)
  3. If \(f\) and \(g\) are differentiable at \(x\) and \(g(x)\ne 0\text{,}\) then \((\frac{f}{g})'(x)=\frac{f'(x)g(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{(g(x))^2}\text{.}\)
