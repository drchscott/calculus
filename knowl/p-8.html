You’ll notice that as the domain values (i.e. the values of \(x\)) approach \(0\text{,}\) the values of \(sin(x)/x\) approach \(1\text{.}\) In fact, if we continue to fill out this chart with values of \(x\) closer and closer to \(0\text{,}\) we’ll notice that \(sin(x)/x\) gets closer and closer to \(1\text{.}\) Of course, to be fair, at the moment we’re only letting \(x\) approach \(0\) from the right hand (positive) side of \(0\text{.}\)
